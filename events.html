The Wait is Over!!

The Summer Concert is Back! Celebrate with us after a two year long hiatus of in person performances. This event is going to be truly special and we have worked incredibly hard during the lockdown on old and new material to share with you all. Tickets are Free!!!

July, 2022

We would like to thank everybody who came to our Summer Concert. It was a very warm day but we enjoyed it immensely and apparently so did you. Once again thank you to our director and all the volunteers for all the work and help. We are now taking a well deserved break but we'll be back rehearsing on the first Saturday of September. Looking forward to greet our new members and if you are still thinking about it, please do get in touch. Have a great summer everyone!!
Mary. K.

June, 2022

We are delighted to welcome our new members Jane, Barry and Lorraine who have joined our ranks this month. We now have 8 sopranos, 7 tenors, 9 altos and 4 bass! A warm welcome also goes to Arlo and Blu, our new reliable 4-legged companions.
John. S.

May, 2022

Hurray! We have confirmed our Summer Concert Venue for July. We're all working hard on the new material which we're hoping will be finished in time for our long awaited performance. we're also going over some pieces which we performed on Jamulus during the pandemic. Hint: 007, James Taylor and Bruckner.
John. S.
